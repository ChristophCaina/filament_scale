01 — Firmware

⚖️
Filament Scale & Winder

Precise filament weight measurement with display, menu navigation, Spoolman & FilaMan integration. The optional winder add-on is detected automatically at boot — no separate firmware needed.

HX711 ST7920 Spoolman FilaMan Winder (auto-detect)

02 — Connect & Flash

firmware: filament-scale
Connect your ESP32 via USB, then click Install.
▶ Install Browser not supported

Requirements

Chrome or Edge browser
USB cable (data, not charge-only)
CP210x / CH340 driver installed
ESP32 in normal boot mode

03 — After Installation

Connect to the ESPHome Hotspot filament-scale that appears after first boot and enter your WiFi credentials.
Open the device in your ESPHome dashboard or wait for Home Assistant to auto-discover it.
Configure your Spoolman / FilaMan IP via substitutions in your local YAML and re-flash OTA.
Calibrate the scale — see calibration.md for step-by-step instructions.
